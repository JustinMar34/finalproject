A Career To Not Pursue

For this page I had a choice of picking an ethical dilemma, a worldwide or community problem, or a career. I decided to pick a career but, rather than pick a career that is successful, I thought I would pick a career that is not successful and one that people should stay away from. After doing some research I have decided that being an architect is one of the worst areas that a person can pursue. One glaring issue with being an architect today is that no one is making new buildings right now, so hat is the need far an architect. People who have majored in architecture have an unemployment rate of 13.9 percent.

The Money Sucks

For as long hours as architects work, they don't make nowhere near what they should. Ina study from US news, the bottom 10% of architects only make roughly $46,000. Only if you are the top 10% make about $125,000. The average wage fot architects was about $76,000 so that is when you have to wonder, is it worth it? Working ridiculous hours to maybe be successful. I don't feel that it is worth it so being an architect sounds like the worst possible job to have.
